Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days. Express shipping is available for 1-2 business days. International shipping times vary by location.

What is your return policy?

We offer 30-day returns on all books in original condition. Rental books can be returned at any time during the rental period.

Do you offer price matching?

Yes, we offer price matching with major competitors. Contact our support team with proof of the lower price.

How do I cancel my order?

Orders can be canceled within 1 hour of placement. After that, please contact customer support for assistance.

Do you have physical stores?

Yes, we have a flagship store in New York City and partner stores across the country. Visit our Stores page for locations.

How do I become a partner?

Interested in becoming a partner store? Please fill out our partnership inquiry form or email partnerships@bookhub.com.
